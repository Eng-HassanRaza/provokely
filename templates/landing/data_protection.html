Data Protection

Last updated: {{ last_updated|default:"October 30, 2025" }}

This page explains how ReviewFlow protects, stores, and processes your data, including data received from connected platforms such as Shopify, Judge.me, and Instagram.

1. Data We Process

  • Store metadata (Shopify) for app installation and configuration.
  • Customer review content (Judge.me) to generate social assets.
  • Connected account identifiers and tokens (Instagram) to publish content you approve.

2. Security

  • Transport security via HTTPS/TLS for all network communications.
  • Credentials stored encrypted at rest. Access is restricted and audited.
  • Least-privilege access for infrastructure and staff.

3. Data Retention

  • Access tokens: retained while integration is active; revoked/removed on disconnect.
  • Generated media and logs: retained for operational needs and analytics; you may request deletion.
  • Backups: short rolling window for disaster recovery.

4. Subprocessors

We may use third-party providers (e.g., hosting, AI generation, analytics) solely to provide the Service. Contracts include data protection obligations.

5. Your Controls

  • Disconnect integrations at any time from settings.
  • Request data export or deletion via support.
  • Configure posting and automation preferences in account settings.

6. Contact

For security reports or data requests, contact us via the dashboard or email support.
