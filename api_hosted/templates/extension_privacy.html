Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

SaaS Validator is a market research tool that helps entrepreneurs validate SaaS ideas by analyzing public Reddit discussions and market trends. Your privacy is our top priority.

Information We Collect

Hosted Service Mode

What we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication only)
  • Project validation data (stored encrypted)
  • Usage statistics (to improve service)
  • Public data from Reddit and Google (scraped, not tracked)

Own API Keys Mode

What we collect:

  • Nothing! All data stays in your browser
  • Your API keys are NEVER sent to our servers
  • No tracking, no analytics, complete privacy

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the validation service
  • Improve our AI models and features
  • Send service-related emails (if opted in)
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security

We NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your validations publicly
  • Track your browsing outside the extension

Data Storage & Security

Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures:

  • All data encrypted at rest and in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • OpenAI API: For AI analysis (see OpenAI Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: For payment processing (see Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Reddit & Google: Public data only, no authentication required

Chrome Extension Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:

  • storage: Save your settings and projects locally
  • tabs: Open Reddit threads and Google searches
  • activeTab: Access current tab when you click the extension
  • sidePanel: Display the extension interface
  • host_permissions:
    • google.com - Extract public search results
    • reddit.com - Extract public discussions
    • provokely.com - Connect to hosted service (optional)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your validation reports
  • Switch between Hosted and Own Keys modes
  • Opt out of analytics and emails

To delete your data:

Email support@provokely.com or uninstall the extension.

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Authentication cookies (required for Hosted Service)
  • Local storage for extension settings
  • No third-party tracking cookies
  • No advertising cookies

Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification (for Hosted Service users)
  • Extension update notes
  • Notice on this page

Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • Email: support@provokely.com
  • Website: https://provokely.com

Legal Compliance

This service complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to transparency and protecting your data.
